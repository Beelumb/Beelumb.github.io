WHO ARE WE?

A second chance. That is what more than a thousand animals that are in rescue centers in the Costa Blanca area ask for. The problem in many cases is that they cannot reach the families who are looking for them.

Tis is a web portal making it easier for you to support and help animals in need or find your next beloved pet. There are three main areas of the portal: ADOPT, where you will find animals that need a new home. HELP and find a local charity.
